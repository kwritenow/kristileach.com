Project: Application for Insurance Agency Staff

I supported UX designers across multiple product teams who coordinated to build digital tools for agency staff adopting a new sales process.

Methods and activities: Research and analysis to inform behavior change from the current sales process to the new sales process and tools:

  • Journey mapping to compare current and proposed flows
  • Heuristic evaluation of the design-in-progress, which became a UX backlog
  • Lean usability testing to evaluate and iterate within sprints
  • Assumption personas co-created with product designers

I also contributed to design for UI refactoring & post-MVP solutioning.

Outcomes: Designers and stakeholders received clear, actionable findings about the risks to adoption. Designers were able to use findings and design guidance to propose interim solutions for MVP and to begin solutioning for post-MVP opportunities. Together, the team reduced the time required for a research cycle (planning to findings shareout) from a week to two days, and design decision-making became more functional: later iterations saw reduced severity of usability issues, and then positive feedback.
